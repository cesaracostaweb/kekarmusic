About

Passionate, artist, bassist, producer and DJ. Cesar Nahuel also known as Kekar, born on November 18, 1987 in the Buenos Aires neighborhood of Once, he grew up all his life in the west of Greater Buenos Aires in the town of Caseros, music captivates and fascinates him at an early age knowing rock and metal, that passion leads him to his first instrument, the bass at the age of 16 and that particularly deep sound caught his attention when he started to discover some clubs in his adolescence. This leads him to quickly find love for the bass lines, kicks and grooves of electronic music and love for subwoofers.

DJ & Producer

Like a DJ his music has played throughout the underground of the city and near of Buenos Aires. Some gigs in Buenos Aires such as Jager, Bloody, The White Room, among others, in Spain Bora Bora Beach Club (Ibiza), El Patio (Ibiza), Murphy`s (Ibiza), @night (Ibiza), in Mexico Hostel3B (Playa del Carmen), Rivera Underground Radio (Playa del Carmen), in Brasil Capitans (Buzios), Dembow Boat Party (Buzios). Kekar ​​is an independent producer, his tracks are available on soundcloud.
